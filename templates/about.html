Empowering Food Security through Intelligence

How we use 17 years of WFP market data to forecast the future of Ghana's agricultural economy.

Our Mission

Global market volatility and shifting climate patterns make it increasingly difficult for farmers, traders, and government agencies to predict crop prices. Our platform was built to bridge this gap using **Predictive Analytics** and **Machine Learning**.

By providing transparent, low-barrier access to price forecasts, we aim to reduce post-harvest waste, stabilize market prices, and ultimately contribute to a more resilient food system in Ghana.

Primary Data Source

Our platform relies on the **World Food Programme (WFP)** Ghana Food Price dataset, covering over 26 commodities across major Ghanaian markets from **2006 to June 2023**.

  • 17+ Years of Historical Pricing
  • Covers Maize, Rice, Cassava, and more
  • Updated Monthly for accuracy

ML Methodology

We utilize a **Decision Tree Regression** model specifically tuned for seasonal variability. By integrating monthly rainfall patterns with historical market trends, our AI accurately forecasts future price floors and ceilings.
